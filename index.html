About Us

In order to compel, one first must engage, which is where we begin all projects.
Whether it’s to educate or motivate (or both), we custom design each video we create with your needs and goals clearly defined.

Our focus is not only to complete jobs within appointed timeframes, but to compose meaningful projects based upon careful research, dynamic creativity, and proven experience.

BPI offers scaleable services to accommodate organizations in reaching specifically targeted audiences. A true “one stop shop”, we host a full suite of 100% in-house production services to take your video from concept to launch in the most efficient and effective manner.

Jay Udavcak

Jay Udavcak created Big Picture. With over 25 years in the production industry, he has worn pretty much every hat involved in the production process (and often borrowing others if it’s a “bad hair day”). He built the company around the concept of being a true partner to our clients, and this guiding principal is echoed in everyone at BPI. In his “spare” time, Jay sings and plays bass in the 9-piece funky rock band Big Bad Juju. He collects vintage microphones and has an admitted “bass guitar addiction”.

Chris Toll

Chris Toll is particularly adept at harnessing the latest technology offered in digital visuals and put it to work for our clients’ needs. He is a DP, an animator, a designer, a certified software instructor, and he knows more about video design than Homer Simpson knows about donuts. Besides being a technological guru, Chris has also proven himself time and again as a creative consultant on the trickiest of projects. He also enjoys electronic music composition as well as photography, where he even sometimes gets to work with his favorite model, his daughter Elsa.

Brett Line

Brett Line's experience ranges from writing, producing videos, editing, composing music and improvisational comedy. He has written for National Geographic and also worked with ABC, FOX, History Channel and Bravo. His short film, "Out Of Service", won Best Picture at the 2013 Clifton Film Festival. He is constantly shooting new shorts with the ultimate goal of making a feature film. In his spare time, he writes bios about himself…poorly.

Jennie Whistler

Jennie Whistler takes multitasking to pretty impressive levels. Having extensive writing, branding, and ad campaign experience on both the agency and publication levels, she is a creative force in the BPI family. She is a scriptwriter and storyboarder, has worked on-set as a grip and PA, coordinates all of our castings, manages our social media, and is a creative force in client meetings, where her perception has proven to be valuable in account management. Jennie has experience as a producer and stage manager for several DC theater productions. She even sang on a new client jingle and has the largest hidden collection of snacks of anyone in our office.
